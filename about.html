About

Hello! I'm Lane, a computer science major at North Carolina State University. I'll be posting my personal projects on this site.

As you can see, most of the software here is made for the TI-84 Plus CE, a color graphing calculator made by Texas Instruments. With its low-level hardware and long history, it is the perfect device to learn programming basics on. This has been a hobby for years, but my focus has mostly shifted to the main platforms for developers.

Currently, I am focusing on coursework (mostly Java, C, Python, etc.), but still work on the occasional hobby project (Like this website!). I also do other random stuff in my blog and socials. Feel free to check back on the pages here for future updates on projects!
