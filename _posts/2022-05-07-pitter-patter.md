I like the rain.

Walking down path I go; sparkling shards of diamonds fall from above.

I trudge on, and they begin to stick to my shoes.

The wind blows, casting another wave.

I am glitter.

And then I step inside, and realize that I am soaking wet.

...

But I like the rain.
